Trials & New Databases from HeinOnline - Updated 9/23/2019

by Caltech Library on September 9th, 2019 in Journals News

An Update: The HeinOnline products have been permanently added to the library collections! Thank you to the faculty and librarians who were able to obtain this great historical content for Caltech Library.

The Library is providing access to some new content from the political science publisher, HeinOnline . Thanks to a faculty request, the Library will provide access to the following HeinOnline databases through September 2019:

  • Acts of the Parliament of Canada (Annual Statutes)
  • Bar Journals
  • Prestatehood Legal Materials
  • Session Laws Library
  • State Attorney General Reports and Opinions
  • State Reports: A Historical Archive - pt. 1
  • State Statutes: A Historical Archive pt. 2

Additionally, because the Library already subscribes to other HeinOnline databases, the publisher is providing free access to the following new databases:

  • GAO Reports and Comptroller General Decisions
  • Gun Regulation and Legislation in America
  • John F. Kennedy Assassination Collection
  • U.S. Congressional Serial Set

Here are some highlights from Caltech Library's HeinOnline holdings:

  • Notes from the Lewis & Clark Expedition - includes maps from the expedition, a summary of findings, and a progress report from Thomas Jefferson. This historic American State Papers document can be found in the new U.S. Congressional Serial Set . See the blog post about this item for more information.
  • The Paris Agreement (2015) - searchable full-text of this 2015 international climate change agreement. This document is in the U.S. Treaties and Agreements Library , which the library has provided for a few years now.
  • The Shortest Article in Law Review History - appears to be a major accomplishment for one Case Western legal professor, who contributed this article to the Journal of Legal Education . This important work can be found in the Law Journal Library , which was recently expanded.
