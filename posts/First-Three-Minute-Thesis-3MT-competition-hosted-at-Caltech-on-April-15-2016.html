First Three Minute Thesis 3MT competition hosted at Caltech on April 15, 2016

by Caltech Library on August 26th, 2016 in Library News

Caltech Library hosted its first Three Minute Thesis 3MTÂ® competition on April 15, 2016.

Created by the University of Queensland , the contest challenges final year PhD students to explain their research in an engaging and clear 3-minute talk, intended for a non-specialist audience. Participants are allowed to use a single, static Power Point slide, shown at the beginning of the talk. Springer Nature generously provided $9,000 in prize money.

Anton Toutov and Utkarsh Mitla were the winners.
