You can find all of our ebooks when you use LibSearch, our discovery service. Look for the [electronic resource] link in the ebook title. You can also use the button or the 'Online Access" link contained in the record. Both links are proxied. If there are ebooks you'd like us to purchase, please use this form and a librarian will get back to you.

Ebook Packages

  • ACLS Humanities E-Books
    Digital collection of over 5,000 Humanities and related Social Sciences full-text titles. Fully searchable with unlimited multi-user access. New titles added each year.
  • ACS Ebooks
    Backfile access to ACS ebooks published between 1949 and 2012, including the ACS Symposium Series and Advances in Chemistry
  • American Astronomical Society (AAS) ebooks
    AAS ebooks are accessible via the IOP platform and cover astronomy and astrophysics subjects, including exoplanets and gamma ray bursts. You're able to download each book as a PDF from the IOP site.
  • AMS ebooks
    The Library has purchased 1000s of math ebooks from the AMS from series such as Graduate Studies in Mathematics, Mathematical Surveys and Monographs, Contemporary Mathematics and at least 7 more.
  • Geological Society of America ebooks
    The Library has access to GSA Field Guides 1(1999)-44(2016), Memoirs 1(1934)-212(2015) and Special Papers 1(1934)-556(2022). All of the volumes are hosted on the GeoScienceWorld platform.
  • CogNet
    CogNet provides online access to reference works, journals and ebooks about the brain and cognitive science.
  • RSC Ebooks
    Over 900 books in a multitude of areas of chemical science.
  • Safari
    38k+ ebooks and 30k+ hours of video tutorials from O'Reilly. Users must register an IMSS email address in the form of *@caltech.edu to access the resource.
  • SIAM ebook program
    Ebooks from SIAM. We have access up until 2015.
  • SPIE ebooks
    Our Library has bought >400 ebooks from SPIE published between 1989-2022. The books include monographs, tutorials, briefs and field guides. The subjects included are broad and stretch from biomedicine to telescope optics.
  • Springer Ebooks
    We have purchased frontfile and backfile collections in seven subject collections through the 2015 imprint year. Subjects include: Math and Statistics, Biomedical and Life Sciences, Chemistry and Materials Science, Computer Science, Earth and Environmental Science, Engineering as well as Physics and Astronomy. These packages include well over 50k ebooks. We have an ongoing subscription to the Math & Stats package (2016+).
  • Synthesis
    Synthesis Digital Library of Engineering and Computer Science, published by Morgan & Claypool, provides access to lectures on research topics in Engineering and Computer Science. We have access to collections 1-12. Now hosted by Springer Nature.
