Lecture Notes in Computer Science

Caltech now has access online to volumes published from 2005 to 2015 of Lecture Notes in Computer Science. We have some older volumes available in print form; just search the library catalog to find them.

If you are looking for a volume that we don't have access to, you have two options:
1) Ask us to buy it in print (since Springer won't sell individual ebooks)
2) Submit a DocuServe request for a specific chapter and we'll get it for you.

Databases/Articles

  • ACM Digital Library
    Fulltext, bibliographic information, abstracts, and reviews for articles published in ACM periodicals & magazines, ACM proceedings & ACM SIG conference proceedings.
    Free iPhone app available.
  • IEEE Xplore
    Provides fulltext access to IEEE transactions, journals, magazines since 1988 as well as IEEE Wiley eBooks
  • Web of Science
    Scientific literature database containing article references from 1900+.
  • MathSciNet
    Site-licensed access to Mathematical Reviews.and Current Mathematical Publications
  • CaltechCSTR
    Caltech Library manages this repository of technical reports on behalf of the Caltech Computer Science Department. Reports are freely available. The CaltechCSTR project is part of CODA .
  • arXiv
    Open access to thousands of e-prints in Physics, Mathematics, Computer Science, Quantitative Biology, Quantitative Finance and Statistics
  • CiteSeerX
    Indexes freely available literature using Autonomous Citation Indexing to create a citation index. ResearchIndex analyzes the computer science literature providing access to technical reports, conference papers, journal articles, books and book chapters.
  • Collection of Computer Science Bibliographies
    A collection of bibliographies of scientific literature in computer science with references mostly to journal articles, conference papers and technical reports.
  • Computing Research Repository (CoRR)
    LANL e-print archive of computing research.
  • io-port.net
    Search Informatics resources
  • DBLP Computer Science Bibliography
    Provides open bibliographic information on major computer science journals and proceedings

Electronic Books

Collections of ebooks

  • Safari
    Full-text access to ebook titles by O'Reilly and other publishers on many programming languages and technologies.
  • Springer Computer Science Ebook package
    We have access to all titles published from 2005 to 2015.
  • IEEE Wiley Ebooks Library
    This eBooks collection contains more than 600 books and spans numerous content areas, including bioengineering, power and energy, and communication technologies, among other growing areas of research.
  • MIT Press eBooks Library
    Titles in the fields of computer science and engineering technologies
  • Synthesis
    Synthesis Digital Library of Engineering and Computer Science, published by Morgan & Claypool, provides access to lectures on research topics in Engineering and Computer Science. We have access to collections 1-12. Now hosted by Springer Nature.
  • SIAM ebook program
    Ebooks from SIAM. We have access through 2022.
