Espacenet

Espacenet contains more than 70 million patent documents from approximately 90 countries and regional patent offices, plus more than 2 million non-patent literature references. Year coverage varies by country. The Espacenet search interface is available in a variety of languages. It also provides access to the INPADOC Legal Status and Patent Family information, and provides a Common Citation Document.

  • Espacenet Search
  • Common Citation Document
    "The Common Citation Document (CCD) application aims to provide single-point access to citation data for the patent applications of the five IP offices (IP5). It consolidates the prior art cited by all participating offices for the family members of a patent application, thus enabling the search results for the same invention produced by several offices to be viewed on a single page."
