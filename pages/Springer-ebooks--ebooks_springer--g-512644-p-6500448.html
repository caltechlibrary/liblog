The Library has purchased 6 subject collections of Springer ebooks through the year 2015. Springer's Math & Statistics ebook package is bought annually. You can find all of these titles in LibSearch , our discovery platform. There is specific package information below.

Archive (-2004) and Contemporary (-2015) Collections

The Lib rary has purchased 1000s of Springer ebooks in the below packages:

Biomedical and Life Sciences, 1888-2015 Chemistry & Materials Science,1893-2015
Computer Science, 1960-2015 Engineering, 1923-2015
Earth & Environmental Science -  1918-2015 Physics & Astronomy - 1891-2018

Current (2016+) Springer ebo oks

The Library has an access program with Springer for newer ebooks. We have access to the above 6 collections as well as the new Intelligent Technologies & Robotics package 2019-2022.

Math & Statistics ebook Package

The Library has a separate agreement and has paid for Springer's Math & Stats ebook collection from 1929 to the present.

Springer MyCopy

Springer also has a MyCopy Program that providers Caltech researchers with the option of purchasing soft copies of these books for $24.99. More information about this program is on this page .
