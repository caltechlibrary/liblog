Free Ebooks

  • 15th World Conference on Nondestructive Testing (Proceedings)
    Selected fulltext of papers in HTML format. Topics include Aeronautics, Computer Processing and Simulation, Nuclear Industry, and Transportation.
  • Aerodynamics for Students
    a web textbook by D.J. Auld of the Department of Aeronautical Engineering, University of Sydney. "Topics relevant to the undergraduate degree in Aeronautical Engineering are covered."
  • Aviation Fuels with Improved Fire Safety: A Proceedings
    Published by the NAP; available fulltext for reading on-line. Search the NAP catalog for similar reports and books.
  • Composite Materials Handbook
    From the US Army Research Laboratory this handbook proposes to be the "primary and authoritative source for statistically-based characterization data of current and emerging polymer matrix, metal matrix, and ceramic matrix composite materials."
  • The Cosmos on a Shoestring
    Rand Corp report detailing the development of small spacecraft within NASA and the DOD.
  • The FEMCI (Finite Element Modeling Continuous Improvement) Book
    Explains and demonstrates different finite element modeling and analysis techniques, including frequency response-vibration and modal weight calculations; links to NASA and Non-NASA finite element modeling sites also provided.
  • The Wilbur & Orville Wright Papers at the Library of Congress
    The online presentation of The Wilbur and Orville Wright Papers at the Library of Congress documents the lives of Wilbur and Orville Wright and highlights their pioneering work which led to them making the world's first powered and sustained flight.
