Frequently Asked Questions About Study Spaces

Who can use library study spaces and how?

  • Caltech students, staff and faculty can use study spaces for collaboration, research, and related activities. To book a space for regularly scheduled classes or special events, please contact us using this form .
  • For Caltech activities that fall outside the Library’s guidelines, refer to other campus locations .

What do I need to use a study space?

  • A valid Caltech UID is required for entry into all Library buildings.

Do I need to reserve a study space?

  • Reservations are recommended.
  • Rooms and spaces are available for walk-in use.
    • If you use a room without a reservation, you will be asked to move to another space to accommodate those with reservations.
  • Rooms are intended for use by groups; Library staff may move a single occupant for a group.
  • Reservations will be forfeited if not present within 15 minutes of the reserved time and are limited to 3 hours per person per day.
    • Special arrangements may be possible, please contact us using this form .

Can I eat or drink in study spaces?

  • Food and covered beverages (i.e. water bottles, to-go cups with a lid) are permitted within Library buildings. Please clean up after yourselves in consideration for others and to prevent pests.
  • Alcohol is not permitted in any library space. Rare exceptions are allowed on a case-by-case basis.

Can I leave personal items in a study room?

Don't leave your personal belongings behind.  If you leave personal belongings in the room unattended, they will be subject to removal.

If we cannot contact you by the end of the day, they will be sent to the Security Office and you will be required to pick them up there.

Outdoor Study Space Guidelines

Outdoor study spaces are available to members of the Caltech community and are located on the east side of the Sherman Fairchild Library and on the terrace of Dabney Library entrance.

  • Food and drinks permitted. Please clean up after yourself.
  • Alcohol is not permitted in any library space. Rare exceptions are allowed on a case-by-case basis.

As with all study spaces, don't leave your personal belongings or trash behind. If personal belongings are found in outdoor spaces, they will be subject to removal and will be sent to the Security Office.
