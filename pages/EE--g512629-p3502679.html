Databases/Articles

  • IEEE Xplore
    Provides fulltext access to IEEE transactions, journals, magazines since 1988. Training videos are available.
  • ACM Digital Library
    Fulltext, bibliographic information, abstracts, and reviews for articles published in ACM periodicals & magazines, ACM proceedings & ACM SIG conference proceedings.
    Free iPhone app available.
  • MathSciNet
    Site-licensed access to Mathematical Reviews.and Current Mathematical Publications
  • Web of Science
    Scientific literature database containing article references from 1900+.
  • CiteSeerX
    Indexes freely available literature using Autonomous Citation Indexing to create a citation index. ResearchIndex analyzes the computer science literature providing access to technical reports, conference papers, journal articles, books and book chapters.
  • PubMed
    Online access to the database of the National Library of Medicine.
    Entries include links to MeSH terms and other NCBI resources, including molecular structure, DNA sequence, and protein sequence databases, as well as to PMC and other OA sources where available. This custom link will also include CaltechConnect functionality to access full text or request via DocuServe.

    This eBooks collection contains more than 600 books and spans numerous content areas, including bioengineering, power and energy, and communication technologies, among other growing areas of research.

  • Synthesis
