Virtual Library Week

2021

The Caltech Library is launching Virtual Library Week starting Monday, April 5 to coincide with National Library Week. You can follow the Library's various social media channels below to learn more and participate in celebrating the Library.

Twitter: twitter.com/caltechlibrary
Instagram: instagram/caltechlibrary
Facebook: facebook.com/caltechlibrary

There will be a Virtual Library Week playlist compiled by our Library staff, Library-inspired Zoom backgrounds, special occasion campus and archival photos, custom #CVLW videos, a Pets of the Caltech Library collage, and more!

—The Caltech Library Staff
