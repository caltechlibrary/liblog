TOPICS FOR PAPER I

Answer one of the following questions in a paper of at least 2000 words (note: please double-space so that I have room for my comments):

1. Based on your reading of Eileen Power's The Peasant Bodo (including her footnotes), Charlemagne’s capitulary De Villis , the Polyptyque from St. Germain des Prés, and the excerpts from the Domesday Book, do you accept Power's reconstruction of ninth-century peasant life? Why or why not?

2. How does The Poem of the Cid portray women? How were they expected to behave? How were men supposed to treat them? Did they play any role in the exercise of social or political power? What might your answers have to say about the poem's intended audience?

3. What role does religion play in The Poem of the Cid ? Do God and/or saints influence the Cid's career and if so, how? How does the Cid interact with them? What about the Church: how does the Cid interact with the Church as an institution? Can you draw any conclusion from your answers about the view of religion held by the poem's intended audience?

4. Guibert of Nogent seems to have had a difficult life. In his memoirs, Guibert explains his life as the journey of a miserable sinner who was constantly losing battles against temptation. Do Guibert’s explanations for the course of his life satisfy you? Or might you, reading between the lines of his account, prefer to explain his life differently?

If you have a different question about that you want to explore in a paper, come see me.
