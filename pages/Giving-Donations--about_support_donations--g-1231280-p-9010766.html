Giving & Donations

A gift to the Library is a contribution to the research of the Institute.

Give Online:

  • Give by mail: Please make checks payable to Friends of the Caltech Library and include any special instructions.

    Caltech Library
    c/o Catherine Geard
    Caltech Hall 2-32
    California Institute of Technology
    Pasadena, CA 91125

  • Create an endowment: An endowment is a fund that supports a specific purpose. Contact Advancement and Alumni Relations for details.

  • Planned giving: You can include the Caltech Library in your estate planning. The Caltech Office of Gift Planning can assist you with developing a plan.
