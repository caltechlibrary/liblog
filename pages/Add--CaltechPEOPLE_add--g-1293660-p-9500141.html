CaltechPEOPLE

CaltechPEOPLE is an index of names of people associated with Caltech in various Library repositories. The list currently includes thesis authors and advisers, current faculty, and a selected—but growing—number of former faculty and researchers.

Library Source Repositories

  • CaltechTHESIS
    —dissertations
  • CaltechAUTHORS
    —publications
  • CaltechDATA
    —data sets and software
  • Caltech Archives
    —papers, manuscripts and other archival material, such as photographs
