GSA Special Papers

We now have access to the Geological Society of America (GSA) Special Papers via GeoScienceWorld.

Our entitlements are from Volume 1 (1934) to Volume 557 (2022).

You can access the content here at GSW's site (if accessing from off-campus, will ask you to proxy in).
