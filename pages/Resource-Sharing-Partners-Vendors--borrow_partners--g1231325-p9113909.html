Resource Sharing Partners/Vendors

DocuServe participates in various networks of organizations who share their resources.

  • Research Library Group (RLG) SHARES Consortium. Over 100 libraries, including:
    • Carnegie Mellon University
    • Columbia University
    • Duke University
    • Library of Congress
    • National Library of Australia
    • Princeton University
    • Royal Library, University of Copenhagen
    • UCLA
    • University of Chicago
    • University of Edinburgh
    • University of Toronto
    • Yale University
  • SCELC Reciprocal Program. Over 60 libraries, including:
    • Claremont Colleges
    • Getty Research Institute
    • Occidental College
    • Pepperdine University
    • USC
  • West Group Access. Over 150 academic libraries, including:
    • Arizona State University
    • California State Universities
    • Oregon State University
    • Stanford University
    • University of California system
    • Washington University of St. Louis
  • RapidILL. Over 200 academic libraries, including:
    • Colorado School of Mines
    • Georgia Institute of Technology
    • Hong Kong University of Science & Technology
    • North Carolina State University
    • Penn State University
    • University of Oregon
  • Reprints Desk. An article on demand service used for RUSH delivery requests .
