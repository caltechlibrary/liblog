Library Resources

  • IEEE Standards
    Browse, search & download ~1,200 IEEE Standards
  • 2001 Annual Book of ASTM Standards
    The Library has the complete set of 2001 ASTM Standards in print available on the 2nd floor of the Sherman Fairchild Library. You can search for a specific standard, or browse collections, at the ASTM Standards & Publications page .
  • ANSI/CFR Standards
    "a one-stop mechanism for access to standards that have been incorporated by reference in the U.S. Code of Federal Regulations (CFR). These standards incorporated by the U.S. government in rulemakings are offered at no cost in “read only” format and are presented for online reading. There are no print or download options."

Educational Resources

  • IEEE Standards University
    Courses, Library, Videos, E-Magazine, Workshops ... plus student grants, "practical ideas from professors", news, etc.
  • ANSI University Outreach Program
    "As the U.S. Member Body of the International Organization for Standardization (ISO), ANSI has been authorized to provide, upon request, complimentary access for students and faculty to selected standards currently available in the ISO collection. If needed, certain standards from the International Electrotechnical Commission (IEC) may also be made available."

Standards

  • MADCAD.com
    A commercial service that lists over 50K codes & standards from: ASCE, ASHRAE, ASTM, AWWA, ASME, ICC, IEEE (see: IEEExplore), NFPA, state codes, etc.
  • Techstreet
    Techstreet is a commercial source for PDF copies of worldwide industry codes and standards.
