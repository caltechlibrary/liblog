Trademarks

  • Assignments on the Web - Trademarks (USPTO)
    Trademarks can (and do) change ownership - this database tracks changes back to 1955.
  • Trademark Electronic Search System (TESS) (USPTO)
    "...searching of existing (USPTO) trademark application and registration information."
  • WIPO Global Brand Database
    Perform a trademark search by text or image in brand data from multiple national and international sources, including trademarks, appellations of origin and official emblems.
  • WIPO Global Design Database
    A world-wide collection of industrial designs data; including WIPO Hague registrations and information from participating national offices.
  • TMQuest
    "...a free searchable trademark database that covers US registered trademarks from 1881 to present."
  • Trademarkia
    Searches more than 6 million trademarks filed since 1870.
