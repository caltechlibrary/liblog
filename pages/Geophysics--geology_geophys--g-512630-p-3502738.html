Earthquake Engineering

The George Housner Earthquake Engineering Collection supports research in the field of structural engineering related to earthquakes, especially the construction of buildings and infrastructure to withstand earthquakes.

Earthquake Engineering is under the Mechanical and Civil Engineering (MCE) department of Engineering and Applied Science (EAS).

For information on Earthquake Engineering, please visit our LibGuide on Earthquake Engineering .
