Chemical Patents in SciFinder

  • SciFinder @ Caltech
    Access restricted to Caltech campus
    Since 1907, has indexed over 3M chemical, biochemical & chemical engineering patents , from ~60 patent authorities (including the Eurasian Patent Organization (EAPO) since 2007, and recently added U.S. ‘chemical’ patents (1808 to 1906).
  • Patent Coverage in SciFinder
    CAS covers patents from around the world. Patent records from nine major patent offices worldwide that meet CAS selection criteria are available online in CAPLUS within two days of the patents' issuance, and fully indexed by CAS scientists in less than 27 days from the date of issue.
