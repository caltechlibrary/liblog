What is the NIH Public Access Policy and what must authors do?

  • How to Comply with the NIH Public Access Policy
    Effective April 7, 2008, all NIH-funded researchers must comply with a new law requiring them to submit copies of peer-reviewed manuscripts accepted for publication and resulting from any NIH funding, to the National Library of Medicine's Pubmed Central.
