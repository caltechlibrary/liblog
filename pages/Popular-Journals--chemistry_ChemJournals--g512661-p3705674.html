Notes About Access

Multidisciplinary

  • PNAS (Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences)

Chemistry Journal Packages

  • American Chemical Society (ACS) Journals
    The Caltech Library subscribes to ALL ACS journals back to Volume 1 EXCEPT for Chemical and Engineering News.
  • Royal Society of Chemistry (RSC) Journals
    The Caltech Library subscribes to all RSC journals back to Volume 1.

General Chemistry

  • Angewandte Chemie International Edition (ACIE)
    Caltech Library provides access back to Volume 1. Please note this title was called "Angewandte Chemie International Edition in English" prior to 1998 (Volume 37). This is NOT the same title as "Angewandte Chemie", which is published in German.
