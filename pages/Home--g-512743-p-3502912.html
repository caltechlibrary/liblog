About 3MT

The Caltech Library is hosting its first 3MT Thesis competition spring 2016 . Created by the University of Queensland , the contest challenges final year PhD students to explain their research in an engaging and clear 3-minute talk , intended for a non-specialist audience. Participants are allowed to use a single, static Power Point slide, shown at the beginning of the talk.  Springer Publishing has generously provided $9000 in prize money:

  • 1st place  -  $5000
  • 2nd place  - $3000
  • People's Choice  -  $1000

15 contestants will be selected from a pool of video submissions to present their talks, live, at an event on April 15th.  See timeline below.

March 18 - 25 : Students submit videos of their talks.  The Library will provide space and equipment for students to create their videos and a means of submission.

March 28 - April 6: Preliminary judging takes place.  A panel of judges here on campus will review the first 60 video submissions and determine which 15 participants continue to the final round.

April 15th: The final round takes place, live! The judging panel for the preliminary round plus two more individuals will vote on the winners. A reception will follow the competition.
