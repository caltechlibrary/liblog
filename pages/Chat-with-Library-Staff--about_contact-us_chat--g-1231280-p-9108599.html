Chat with Library Staff

The Library's chat reference service—aka "Ask the Library"—is open from 8:00 am–12:00 pm and 1:00–5:00 pm Monday–Friday. Patrons should feel free to ask any questions they think the library can answer—managing library accounts, item renewals, current library hours, resources for research, library fines, equipment rental, study spaces, library policies, TechHub services, and much more.
