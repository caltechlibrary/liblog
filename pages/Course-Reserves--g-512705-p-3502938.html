Other Recommended Reference Resources

  • Comprehensive Organic Synthesis, 2nd Edition (2014)
    Fully revised and updated, this new set forms an essential reference work for all those seeking information on the solution of synthetic problems.
    * Contains more than 170 articles across nine volumes, including detailed analysis of core topics such as bonds, oxidation, and reduction.
    * Includes more than10,000 schemes and images.
    * Fully revised and updated; important growth areas—including combinatorial chemistry, new technological, industrial, and green chemistry developments—are covered extensively.
  • Modern Physical Organic Chemistry (Dougherty & Anslyn)
    Print version available in the collection. If you have trouble obtaining this book, please contact me!
