Financial Analysis with MatLab

Matlab provides a central tool for implementing complicated quantative financial models and is available to Caltech students for free through https://software.caltech.edu/ .  Here are some references on financial modeling with Matlab:

  • Simulation and Optimization in Finance: Modeling with MATLAB, @Risk, or VBA
  • Stochastic Simulation and Applications in Finance with MATLAB Programs
  • Strategic Asset Allocation in Fixed-Income Markets: A MATLAB-based User's Guide
  • MATLAB with Applications to Engineering, Physics and Finance

Coursera MOOC's

  • Financial Markets - Bob Shiller
    An overview of the ideas, methods, and institutions that permit human society to manage risks and foster enterprise.
  • Introduction to Finance - Gautam Kaul
    This course introduces frameworks and tools to measure value and help in financial decision making, both for corporate and personal assets.
  • Financial Engineering and Risk Management Part I - Martin Haugh and Garud Iyengar
    This course provides an introduction to various classes of derivative securities and we will learn how to price them using "risk-neutral pricing".
