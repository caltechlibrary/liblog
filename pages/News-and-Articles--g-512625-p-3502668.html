Caltech Connect

Whenever you're looking for articles in databases that don't give you full text, look for this icon, or the words "Caltech Connect", for help in determining whether we have access to that article.

Business Article Databases

  • Business Source Complete This link opens in a new window
    • Index and abstract database for scholarly business journals back to 1886. Searchable cited references are provided for more than 1,300 journals.
    • Includes newspapers, trade publications, and company and industry information and reports.
    • Search by NAICS code/Industry
    • Tutorial available here.
  • EconLit
    Major database devoted to the subject of economics with citations to journal articles, books, and working papers on economics.
  • Nexis Uni This link opens in a new window
    Major full-text resource for business information providing up to date information from over 4,000 business, news, legal and medical periodicals, newsletters and newswires.

Non-Business Articles

Many other article databases (not specifically business-focused) can be accessed at Caltech and may contain helpful information.

  • Web of Science
    Covers articles in science, social science and humanities.
  • PubMed
    30 million citations for biomedical literature from MEDLINE, life science journals, and online books from 1946 to present (based on MEDLINE), with selected coverage back to 1809. From the United States National Library of Medicine at the National Institutes of Health.
    Entries include links to MeSH terms and other NCBI resources, including molecular structure, DNA sequence, and protein sequence databases, as well as to PMC and other OA sources where available. This custom link will also include CaltechConnect functionality to access full text or request via DocuServe.

Newspaper Databases

  • Newspaper Source Plus
    Includes 860 full-text newspapers, 35 million full-text articles, and 857,000 television and radio news transcripts.
  • Regional Business News This link opens in a new window
    Coverage of more than 80 regional business publications covering all metropolitan and rural areas within the United States.
