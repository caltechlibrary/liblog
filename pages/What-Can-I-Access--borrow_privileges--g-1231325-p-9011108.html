Identification Requirements

Learn about the type of identification you need and whether you need to sign in to access Library spaces.

Caltech Students, Faculty, and Staff

All faculty, trustees, visiting faculty, post-docs, staff, students, and summer students must have a current Caltech ID for tap access.

Other Groups

Groups listed below only have access to Sherman Fairchild Library. Visitors will be required to fill out our Visitors Form prior to arrival and present required ID when they check-in at SFL Service Desk upon arrival. Effective Fall 2022, subject to change.
