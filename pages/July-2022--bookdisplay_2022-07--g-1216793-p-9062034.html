Good Reads

  • I Am Malala by Malala Yousafzai; Christina Lamb (As told to)
    Call Number: LC2330 .Y69 2013 (c.1)
    ISBN: 9780316322409
    Publication Date: 2013-10-08
    A MEMOIR BY THE YOUNGEST RECIPIENT OF THE NOBEL PEACE PRIZE
    I Am Malala is the remarkable tale of a family uprooted by global terrorism, of the fight for girls' education, of a father who, himself a school owner, championed and encouraged his daughter to write and attend school, and of brave parents who have a fierce love for their daughter in a society that prizes sons. I AM MALALA will make you believe in the power of one person's voice to inspire change in the world.
  • Nelson Mandela by Mary Benson; Bishop Desmond Tutu (Foreword by)
    ISBN: 0393303225
    Publication Date: 1986-03-01
    A detailed biography with great amounts of background material that ends before the civil rights leader was released from detention.
  • Mandela's way : fifteen lessons on life, love, and courage by Richard Stengel; Nelson Mandela
    ISBN: 9780307460691
    Publication Date: 2009
    "Time" magazine editor Stengel, who collaborated with Mandela on his bestselling autobiography, distills Mandela's wisdom into 15 vital life lessons that have the power to deepen lives.
