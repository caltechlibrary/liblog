Whenever you're looking for articles in databases that don't give you full text, look for this icon, or the words "Caltech Connect", for help in determining whether we have access to that article.

Top Resources

  • Business Source Complete This link opens in a new window
    • Index and abstract database for scholarly business journals back to 1886. Searchable cited references are provided for more than 1,300 journals.
    • Includes newspapers, trade publications, and company and industry information and reports.
    • Search by NAICS code/Industry
    • Tutorial available here.
  • Entrepreneurial Studies Source
    Has full text for periodicals, reference books, case studies, and thousands of company profiles.
  • Nexis Uni This link opens in a new window
    Major full-text resource for business information providing up to date information from over 4,000 business, news, legal and medical periodicals, newsletters and newswires. Under "Advanced Search" then "Company and Financial", access Analyst Reports, Company Profiles, Mergers and Acquisition info, and more.

Tips

  • Public companies will have much more information available; private companies can be very secretive.
  • Analyst reports and market research reports are very expensive, but may have abstracts available for free.
