Equipment

Caltech Library TechLab currently has 13 CraftUnique CraftBot Plus FFF/FDM 3D printers in operation. We use exclusively 1.75mm PLA filament, unless by special request.

CraftUnique Craftbot Plus

  • Build Volume: 250 x 200 x 200 mm / 9.8 x 7.9 x 7.9 in
  • Platform: Removable aluminum plate w/ PEI surface on heated platform, up to 110 °C
  • Extruder: 1, up to 260 °C
  • Nozzle Size: 0.4 mm
  • Finest Layer Resolution: ≥ 100 micron (0.1 mm)
  • Filament Diameter: 1.75mm
  • Compatible Materials: ABS, PLA, T-Glase/PETG, Nylon, NinjaFlex, Laywood, specialty "-fill" type PLA (bronze, copper, etc) and more

For More Info: https://craftbotusa.com/craftbot
