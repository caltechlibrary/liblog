Patent, Trademark and Copyright Issues

The Research Affairs Group of the Office of General Counsel handles all intellectual property-related issues for the Caltech campus and JPL. Patent, trademark and copyright issues may be directed to that group, as well as questions regarding federal (Bayh-Dole) invention reporting requirements.

Copyright Tutorial : This contains information on copyright basics, as well as related issues that may be of interest to the Caltech community. Links to additional references are also provided within each section of the tutorial. If, after reading the materials, you still have questions, please contact us.

Notices of Alleged Copyright Infringement: Please direct all notices of alleged copyright infringement on a Caltech-hosted site to the link shown.

Staff: The intellectual property attorneys in the Research Affairs group are Oral Caglar , Chantal D'Apuzzo and Adam Cochran .
