Course Reserves

List of Current Course Reserves

The location of the item will be listed in the catalog. Books may have different circulation policies depending on the instructor of the course:

  • SFL OPEN RESERVE items are located on the first floor of the Sherman Fairchild Library in the browsable stacks behind the Reference Desk. They can be used indefinitely within the library, or checked out for three hours at a time for use outside the library.
  • SFL CIRC-RESERVE items can be requested at the Book Check-Out desk on the first floor of the Sherman Fairchild Library. They can be checked out for three hours at a time and may be taken outside of the library.

If you are an instructor interested in placing materials (including electronic) on reserve for a course, please visit our Course Reserves LibGuide .
