Conference Papers/Reports/Preprints/Abstracts

  • arXiv
    Open access to e-prints in Physics, Mathematics, Computer Science, Quantitative Biology, Quantitative Finance and Statistics
  • Bio Web of Conferences
    BIO Web of Conferences is an open access venue for archiving conference proceedings in biology.
  • British Library. Reports
    Access to records for approximately 4.9 million unrestricted reports available for public use, including:
    British research and development reports
    US agencies, such as DOD, DOE, ERIC, NASA, NTIS
    International agencies, such as INIS, ESA, FAO, EU
  • British Library. The Conference Collections
    The British Library conference index contains the records of 400,000 conference proceedings held in stock, with 16,000 new records added each year.
  • Ecological Society of America
    ESA's video channel
  • EJP Web of Conferences
    Open-access proceedings in pure and applied physics, including materials science, physical biology, physical chemistry, and complex systems.
  • Elsevier Procedia
    Procedia are e-only conference proceedings published in dedicated open-access issues on ScienceDirect. There are a wide variety of subject series, including: Energy; Chemistry; Environmental Sciences; as well as conferences sponsored by various societies (IUTAM, IERE, AASRI, etc.)
  • ESAIM(European Series in Applied and Industrial Mathematics): Proceedings
    ESAIM Proceedings includes colloquia, seminars, summer schools, etc., in all areas of applied mathematics
  • IOP Conference Series: Earth and Environmental Science (EES)
    An Open Access publication that complements a community web site [environmentalresearchweb.org] and the open access journal Environmental Research Letters.
  • IOP Conference Series: Materials Science and Engineering (MSE)
    As an open-access title, MSE will make conference papers available to material scientists, physicists, chemists and engineers
  • Journal of Physics: Conference Series
    An open-access journal with conference proceedings in each volume, from 2004+
  • Web of Conferences
    Web of Conferences is a scientific conference portal that collects both open access & subscription based conference proceedings series and journals published by EDP Sciences.

Conference Papers / Reports - Indexing/Abstracting

  • E-Print Network
    "A gateway to over 35,300 websites and databases worldwide, containing over 5.5 million e-prints in basic and applied sciences ... of interest to DOE."
  • IEEE Conference Proceedings
    Full coverage from 1988 with selected content back to the 1950s.
  • Information Bridge: DOE Scientific and Technical Information
    Access to over 210K full-text documents and bibliographic citations of DOE research reports mainly from 1991+. Word clouds can be viewed by hovering over report titles or PDF icon.
  • International Nuclear Information System
    The online archive of the IAEA containing ~3M records from journals, grey literature, and internet documents ... many with access to the full text.
  • NASA Technical Reports Server (NTRS)
    The NTRS database has indexing/abstracting for over 500,000 aerospace citations (including ‘chemistry and materials’) with over 200,000 that are online full-text. In addition, there are over 500,000 images and videos. NTRS includes both NASA (1958+) and NACA (1916-1958) reports, as well as the NASA Image eXchange (1900+), and is freely available.
  • PapersFirst
    Index of papers presented at conferences worldwide from 1993 to present ... based on items received by the British Library Supply Centre. BLLD Shelfmarks can be searched at https://www.bl.uk/
  • SciTech Connect
    Incorporating the contents of the DOE Information Bridge and the Energy Citations Database, with an innovative semantic search tool which includes faceting, in-document search, word clouds, and personalization. There are over 2.5 million citations (1.4 million journal articles(364,000 with DOIs), including 313,000 full-text DOE reports.
