Welcome new SFP students!

For all SFP students who wish to access library resources from off-campus, as well as request articles and book chapters through DocuServe (Interlibrary Loan), please ensure you have an active access.caltech account!

Non-Caltech SFP students can check out up to 5 books from the Caltech Library, and may use our mediated Techlab (3D printing) service. For more details please see our COVID-19 Services page !
