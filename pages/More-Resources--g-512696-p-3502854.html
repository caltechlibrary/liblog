What's In SciFinder?

  • Core Journal List
    Bibliographic information and abstracts for all articles in more than 1,500 key scientific journals are added to CAplus within 7 days. The list is sorted alphabetically by the full journal title and also includes the journal abbreviation, CODEN, and publication frequency.
  • CAS Content At A Glance
    Latest counts for CAS REGISTRY, CAplus, CASREACT, CHEMLIST, CHEMCATS. CIN, MARPAT, plus 'How do we count'. Also provides details on content and coverage.
