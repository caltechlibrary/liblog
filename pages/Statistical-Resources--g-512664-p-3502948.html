Caltech Library Government Documents

Caltech does not have a separate and discrete publicly-accessible U.S. government documents collection. Caltech is selective and integrates the material into the overall collection and in recent years puts priority on electronic resources.

For assistance with obtaining government information:

  • Use this guide
  • Submit your question . We will respond as soon as we can.
  • Text us: 626-587-4833
  • Search for an answer to your question in our FAQ.

Statistics Sources

  • Agriculture Statistics
    Agricultural Statistics is published each year to meet the diverse need for a reliable reference book on agricultural production, supplies, consumption, facilities, costs, and returns.
  • Bicentennial Edition: Historical Statistics of the United States: Colonial Times to 1970 - PART 1
  • Census Data Mapper
    The Census Data Mapper is a web mapping application intended to provide users with a simple interface to view, save and print county-based demographic maps of the United States. The data are from the 2010 Census.
  • Census Bureau - Publications by Topics
    View the list of publications by year. This page displays the topics covered by the publications released by the U.S. Census Bureau.
  • Bicentennial Edition: Historical Statistics of the United States - Colonial Times to 1970 - PART 2
  • FedStats
    statistics from more than 100 agencies
  • Health US
    Health, United States is an annual report on trends in health statistics.
  • Inter-university Consortium for Political and Social Research - ICPSR
    Inter-University Consortium for Political and Social Research -- lots of data, including a lot of US government agency data
  • Statistical Abstracts of the United States
    Statistical Abstract data present here ranges from our most recent edition to the historical abstracts compiled throughout the decades. Some of the data were scanned as an effort to make historical abstract information available to the public. The display of data will continue as historical records become available.
  • USDA Economics and Statistics System
    A collaborative project between Albert R. Mann Library at Cornell University and several agencies of the U.S. Department of Agriculture. The system contains nearly 2500 reports and datasets. These materials cover U.S. and international agriculture and related topics.
  • Vital Statistics of the United States
    Annual reports that present detailed vital statistics data, including natality, mortality, marriage and divorce. These reports are available for download. Some are available as bound volumes in many large public and university libraries.
