TOPICS FOR PAPER II

Answer one of the following questions in a paper of at least 2000 words (note: please double-space so that I have room for my comments):

1. Looking over our sources from the Second and the Third Crusade, how do you see relations developing between the Frankish settlers in Palestine and successive waves of newcomers? [note: be careful to explore to what degree your picture is influenced by the different identities, perspectives, and agendas of your sources]

2. Using both Muslim and Christian sources, draw me a picture of Saladin. What kind of person do you think he was, what was his character like, what motivated him, etc.? [note 1: be careful to explore to what degree your picture is influenced by the different identities, perspectives, and agendas of your sources] [note 2: it would probably help to read all of the texts in the Arab Historians reader on Saladin, not just the ones we discussed in class]

3. Read the rest of Geoffrey of Villehardouin’s Conquest of Constantinople . Once the leaders of the Fourth Crusade decide to stay in Byzantium, what happens to the Crusade? What happens to the Crusaders’ goals, as Villehardouin depicts them? How does Villehardouin explain and justify the fact that the Crusader princes are now permanently occupying and ruling the former Byzantine Empire? How does he explain and justify what they do as they struggle to maintain their rule? Or does he even try?

4. Read the rest of Geoffrey of Villehardouin’s Conquest of Constantinople . According to Villehardouin, once the Latins establish themselves as the rulers of Byzantium, how do they stay in power and maintain their rule in the face of internal and external threats? Would you answer this question the same way he does? Why?

If you have a different question about that you want to explore in a paper, come see me.
