Purpose and Audience

One of the goals of our webpages is to enable our users to access library information quickly and easily to complete their tasks. Another is to promote library resources, services, people, and information.

Our primary audience is our students and faculty. Our secondary audience includes other affiliates, such as staff, post-docs, community members, donors, library staff, prospective employees and students, and administration.

Guide Types

LibGuides CMS features 6 guide types that are fixed:

General Purpose

Use General Purpose for common web pages describing library services, policies, events, etc.

Subject

Use Subject for any guides related to Caltech divisions (BBE, etc.) or resource types (standards, ebooks, images, patents, etc.)

Topic

Use Topic for guides that don't fit the other categories, like the Diversity & Inclusion Guide.

Course

Use Course for guides tailored to specific courses.

Internal

Use Internal type if you do not want your guide to appear publicly.

Template

Use the Template guide to create landing pages or specific types of pages, like events or landing pages.
