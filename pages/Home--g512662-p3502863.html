  • Annalen der Physik - Series & Total volume numbering, Editors
    "Vol. = Volume = total volume count, with a continous counting of all volumes since 1799
    Band (German for Volume) = Volume Counting within a series (German "Reihe" or "Folge"),
    Folge = series, there exist eight series until today,
    Reihe = within a Folge (German for series) there can be sub-series (= Reihen), see for example Poggendorff's Annalen from 1824 onwards. PLEASE NOTE: To distinguish between the total volume count numbering and the classical (historic) citation form, the term Band and Serie has been adapted for use within a Folge whereas Volume (as total volume number) is used as modern citation form. Herausgeber (Hrsg.) is here translated as Editor-in-Chief, or shortly E-i-C
    Redakteur (Red.) or Mitredakteur is translated as Editor (Ed.) "
  • CAS Source Index (CASSI) Search Tool
    Quickly identify or confirm journal titles and ISO Standard Abbreviations for publications indexed by CAS since 1907, including journals, conference proceedings, technical reports, etc. Search by Title, Abbreviation, CODEN, ISBN, or ISSN.
    Note that there is a limit of 50 answers per search. It compliments the print CASSI [Z5523 A524] which includes library holdings, etc.
