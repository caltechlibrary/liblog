Introduction

Dimensions is a searchable database of publications , citations, grants , patents and clinical trials , the relationships between them, and a variety of analytical views . It provides both keyword search and the ability to filter by:

  • publication year (e.g. 2018 )
  • researcher (e.g. William Goddard )
  • country (of author afillliation)
  • research organization (e.g. Caltech )
  • funder (e.g. NASA )
  • field of research (e.g. Astronomical and Space Sciences )
  • publication type
  • source/journal title (e.g. Physical Review D )
  • specific journal lists (e.g. PubMed )
  • open access (from publishers or OA repositories)

To find and select a filter click More below a list of facets and enter as much of the name as you know. To set the filter click Limit to :

Keywords can be added to the search. The keyword search is on the full text, abstract only, or title and abstract only.
