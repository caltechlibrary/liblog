About the OTT Innovation Collection

The OTT Innovation Collection contains titles that discuss technology commercialization and entrepreneurship, focusing on areas such as competitive strategy, startup guidance, management, and marketing. The Collection is maintained and developed by the Caltech Office of Technology Transfer (OTT) and the Caltech Library.

Print titles in the Collection are located on the ninth floor of the Millikan building (The Lookout) - links below will show the library catalog page indicating availability. [Ebook] titles may be accessed via links on this page - please note users must have access.caltech credentials in order to view content.

Startup Guide

  • Engineering entrepreneurship from idea to business plan : a guide for innovative engineers and scientists (Swamidass, 2016)
    The key features of this reference enable readers to sharpen their new idea, turn an idea into a commercial product, conduct patent search and complete a provisional patent application, and collect requisite data and prepare a business plan based on a carefully selected business model. Supporting materials are provided on the book's extensive website (www.engineer-entrepreneur-book.com/).
