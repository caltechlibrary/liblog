About This Guide

Welcome! On this guide you will find links to resources mentioned in the Hixon Writing Center's resource handout "Approaching the Diversity Statement".

Accessing resources:

  • Physical resources in the Library collection may be accessed according to current Library COVID-19 procedures .
  • Items we do not currently own may be requested through DocuServe . Valid access.caltech credentials are required to use this service.
  • Access to electronic resources (books or journal articles) is limited to members of the Caltech community. Freely available resources will be indicated.
    • Detailed instructions for accessing EBSCO and ProQuest ebooks can be found on our Ebooks Guide .
